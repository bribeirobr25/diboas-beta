Cryptocurrency might seem complicated, but it's actually quite simple once you understand the basics. Let's start with what you need to know to make your first investment confidently.

What is Cryptocurrency?

Think of cryptocurrency as digital money that exists only on computers. Just like you can send an email instantly to anyone in the world, you can send cryptocurrency to anyone, anywhere, at any time.

Key Point: Cryptocurrency is just digital money that isn't controlled by any bank or government. It's like having cash, but digital.

The Big Four: Your Starting Options

While there are thousands of cryptocurrencies, we recommend starting with these four proven options:

  • Bitcoin (BTC) - The original and most trusted cryptocurrency
  • Ethereum (ETH) - The platform for smart contracts and applications
  • Solana (SOL) - Fast and low-cost transactions
  • Sui (SUI) - Next-generation blockchain technology

Why Start with Just $10?

You don't need thousands of dollars to begin. Starting small helps you:

  • Learn without risking significant money
  • Build confidence gradually
  • Understand how the market works
  • Develop good investment habits
